2025 Donor Levels/Benefits
Contributor - less than $2,500 - "Contributor" recognition on the website. Supporter - $2,500 - $5,000 - Above plus in-depth quarterly Newsletter recognition. Sponsor - $5,000 - $10,000 - Above tiers and social media mentions with company spotlight. Advocate - $10,000 - $25,000 - Trail sponsorship/signage plus above tiers. Protector - $25,000 - $50,000 - Above with additional media and trail day for your entity. Visionary - $50,000+ - Naming rights for approved trail system (needs funding for buildout).
The above reference tiers are suggested, and we are willing to work with supporters to ensure their needs are met in concert with our over-arching vision.
Donate

GTF is a self-funded effort working with the City of Georgetown, several businesses and organizations in Georgetown, and private land owners to connect a series of natural surface trails in the city limits. All proceeds are GREATLY appreciated and will be used toward building and maintaining trails curated in the GTF master plan.
