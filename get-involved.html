Volunteer

We're thrilled that you're interested in joining our passionate community of trail enthusiasts dedicated to preserving and enhancing the natural beauty of our trails. Whether you're an avid hiker, biker, nature lover, or simply someone who believes in the importance of outdoor spaces, we have meaningful volunteer opportunities that will allow you to contribute to the betterment of our trails and create lasting memories.

Join us in making a difference on the trails! Make sure you are signed up for our newsletter so you do not miss volunteer opportunities. Together, we can ensure the trails remain a cherished resource for generations to come. Thank you for considering volunteering with GTF!

Trail Maintenance

We need your help in maintaining these trails so GTF can focus on building new trails for the community. If you are willing to adopt a section of trail, please reach out to us and we will get you set up with instructions and tools. Most of the needs are mowing, weed eating, and small branch removal to keep the trails safe and encourage traffic.

Group Ride

Join us for our weekly group ride. All abilities are welcome! Bring a working bike, helmet, and water. We typically do a 10-12 mile loop sampling all the GTF trails in town and end at 600 Degrees Marketplace on the square for food and drink.

When: Every Thursday at 6:30pm (weather and holiday dependent)

Where: Katy Crossing Trailhead in San Gabriel Park (Location)

Donate

GTF is a self-funded effort working with the City of Georgetown, several businesses and organizations in Georgetown, and private land owners to connect a series of natural surface trails in the city limits. All proceeds are GREATLY appreciated and will be used toward building and maintaining trails curated in the GTF master plan.
